Instant Malayalam

Chrome Extension Firefox Add-on

Double-click any word to view its Malayalam definition in a pop-up. Click on "കൂടുതൽ വ്യാഖ്യാനങ്ങൾ ->" in the pop-up to see complete definitions on a new tab. This extension uses the Olam online Malayalam dictionary API.

It is in beta stage. Please provide your feedback at arunkumarv31 at gmail dot com.
